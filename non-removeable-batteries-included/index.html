Non-removeable Batteries Included

24 October 2014

Smartphones are awesome. There is no better device on the planet to avoid real human interaction than a smartphone. If you’re using an iPhone 4S or newer, there’s a good chance you’re on your way to updating your software to the latest and greatest iOS8. If you manage to get the update installed, you might enjoy the seemingly minor addition of being able to tell what apps are sapping your battery. Check it out by following: Settings >General >Usage >Battery Usage. Lets face it, if we run out of juice too quickly, we might have to plug-in to the real world.
